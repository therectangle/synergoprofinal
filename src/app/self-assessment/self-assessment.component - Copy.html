  • Keep your clipboard or notebook on top of a pillow placed on your lap for a stable and higher postion.
  • Writing at a table is preferred whenever possible to reduce bending over, lesser risk of back and neck strain.
  • A document holder can be used to hold up a magazine or pieces of paper for easier viewing without the need to hold them up.
